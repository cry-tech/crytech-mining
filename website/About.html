About CryTech

Who we are

After researching in the field of blockchain and crypto-currencies, we found its future bright and promising. In May 2017, we formed the CryTech team with three co-founders and started working in the fields of trading, making robots and mining crypto-currencies. Currently, our team has over ten members and is still growing.

What we do

We are active in various aspects of blockchain and crypto-currencies, including: mining, trading (automated trading, technical analysis, fundamental analysis), creating new blockchain products and services, etc.

Our vision

We believe that blockchain and its products, including crypto-currencies will play a very important role in the world and we try to be part of this Movement.
